ABOUT ME

Welcome to Takuya Matsumoto's personal homepage. I am a physicist aiming at a unified understanding of the behavior of electrons in solids, both experimentally and theoretically. I am interested in material design from first-principles calculations and quantum transport properties using cryogenic measurement techniques.
